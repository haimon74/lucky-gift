OBJECTIVE
Implement Step 2 of the wizard: the message editor with the occasion visual and a live full gift card preview that updates as the user types.

DELIVERABLES

apps/web/

1. components/wizard/WizardStep2.tsx:

     - Large card (full width, ~200px tall) with gradient background from occasion.gradientColors

     - Smooth fade-in animation when rendered
   - **Message Editor**:
     - Textarea (dark themed, from @lucky-gift/ui)

     - Simulates a gift card/envelope at mobile width (~320px)
     - Gradient background matching occasion
     - Occasion emoji (large)
     - Occasion display name
     - Message text (live, truncated after 120 chars in preview with "...")
     - "from: [senderName or 'You']" at bottom
     - Gold "Open Gift" button mock (not clickable, just visual)
     - Label above: "Preview — how recipient sees it"
   - Layout: 2-column on desktop (editor left, preview right), stacked on mobile

2. components/GiftCardPreview.tsx (reusable):
   - Standalone component used in Step 2 and Step 1 mini-preview
   - Props: occasionKey, message, senderName, size ('mini' | 'full')
   - 'mini' size: compact preview ~160px tall for Step 1
   - 'full' size: full preview ~400px for Step 2
